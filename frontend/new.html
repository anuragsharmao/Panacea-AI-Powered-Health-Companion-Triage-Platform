AI Core
AI Evaluation Engine · NLTK · Sentence‑BERT · TF‑IDF · Celery worker
Step 1
Preprocess
  • Lowercase, tokenize (NLTK)
  • Stopword removal, lemmatize
  • Applied to student & model answer
Step 2
Gibberish filter
  • WordNet lookup per token
  • Min word count & language check
  • If fails → score=0 instantly
Step 3
TF‑IDF + cosine
  • Vectorize student answer
  • Compare with all alt_answers[ ]
  • Pick best cosine score
Step 4
Sentence‑BERT
  • Semantic embeddings
  • Cosine similarity (all alt_answers)
  • Handles synonyms & rephrasing
Step 5 · New
Rubric criterion check
  • For each rubric criterion
  • BERT similarity per criterion
  • Award partial marks independently
Step 6 · New
Length normalisation
  • Compare vs expected_length
  • Penalty if <40% or >300%
  • Prevents padding or concise penalisation
Score fusion formula
final = (0.4×cos_best + 0.6×BERT_best + rubric_total) × length_penalty × max_marks
BERT weighted higher for semantic understanding; rubric adds partial bonus.
